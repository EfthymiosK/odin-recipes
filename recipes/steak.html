tuna steak

description

This tuna steak recipe uses a tangy marinade made with orange juice, soy sauce, and garlic for a wonderful taste.

ingredients

  • Orange juice
  • Soy sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh parsley
  • Fresh oregano
  • Fresh garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Make the marinade
  2. Marinate the tuna steaks.
  3. Grill the marinated tuna steaks, basting often, to desired doneness.
